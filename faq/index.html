Questions from Customers

What is ProtoExchange?

ProtoExchange provides access to top Hardware Engineers for consultation and development.

How does ProtoExchange work?

Subscribe to ProtoExchange for assistance from engineers selected for their expertise in your unique requirements. We consider many factors when matching clients with engineers, including CAD expertise, experience developing similar products, engineer availability and more.

How do you handle NDAs?

We can supply NDAs that are required for a project for a one-time fee. We record signings through an electronic auditing system for easy and transparent use.

How are Engineers curated?

Engineers apply to join ProtoExchange. Members of our staff review each individual application and cross-reference with professional and social profiles (such as Github, Linkedin, StackOverflow, etc.). We further evaluate their ability to communicate effectively, efficiently, and professionally.

What are the advantages of ProtoExchange?

ProtoExchange enables you to solve urgent technical challenges faster by connecting you with experienced engineers. Engineering assistants are great for those in need of quick design review, advice or mentor-ship. ProtoExchange Assistants can augment your existing engineering team, and together we can tackle technical challenges beyond your in house capabilities. Hardware development is expensive; faulty design can lead to costly manufacturing mistakes. Fresh eyes can save you time and money.

How does matching work?

We review your engineering requirements and match you with the best engineers; we focus on experience, expertise, location, availability.

What if I prefer a different engineer than recommended?

We host introductory calls to ensure that the engineer we’ve chosen is the correct match. If not, we’ll continue to search for and recommend engineers until you’re satisfied.

Can I change engineers in the middle of a subscription?

If your engineering needs change, we're happy to match you with a new engineer with the expertise you are seeking at any time.

Questions from Engineers

How is payment handled?

Once you have been matched with a client you will be paid each month based on subscription plan the client has selected.

Is work filed under 1099?

Work is filed under 1099. All work is considered contract work.

What can I do to improve my profile?

One of the best ways of improving your profile is linking external accounts (Github, Linkedin, etc.). External accounts help us verify your portfolio. Plus, we love interacting with our engineers over social media!

Does ProtoExchange provide any CAD licenses?

While we do not provide CAD licenses, we do provide a development environment to help organize communication between you and the client. Each engineer is responsible for their own CAD tools.

Can I decline a request for assistance?

Yes. If a client's request is not of interest to you or you simply do not have the time to successfully complete the work, we’d prefer you decline the request! We do not penalize you for declining work.
